~ The Family of Clemens and Henrietta (Schrader) Meyerhoff ~

Clemens Henry Meyerhoff was the first child of Carl and Minnie Meyerhoff, born October 2, 1873, in Wine Hill, IL. He married Henrietta Louise Schrader (1875-1961) on August 12, 1900, in Wine Hill. Together they had five children listed below. Their family moved to Jerome County in Idaho in 1914 along with his younger brother Henry. Clemens passed away on January 30, 1944, in Eden ID, at the age of 70.

Henrietta Louise Schrader was one of the younger children of Dorothea Sophia Conradine Schulze (1835-1913) and Johann Julius Christoph Schrader (1824-1895). Her family arrived in New Orleans in 1849 and moved to Southern Illinois shortly after. After celebrating 43 years of marriage and 17 years after the death of her husband, Henrietta passed away on January 13, 1961, at the age of 85.

The couple were buried in Sunset Memorial Park Cemetery in Twin Falls, Idaho. According to my family tree they had five children, 17 grandchildren, and 12 great-grandchildren (although I believe that last number to be higher).

  1. Augest Meyerhoff (1902-????)
    2. Augest was the first son of Clemens and Henrietta, born around 1902. My only record of him comes from the 1910 U.S. census, where it states he was born in Illinois and was eight years old at the time it was taken.

  2. Olga Christina (Meyerhoff) Ehlers (1902-1990)
    3. Olga was the second child and first daughter of Clemens and Henrietta, born February 20, 1902, in Perry County, IL. She married Walter Henry Ehlers (1894-1980) on April 12, 1923, in Twin Falls County, ID. Together they had three children: Elmer Walter Ehlers (1924-2010), Bernice (Ehlers) Hiatt (1927-2021), and Marvin Harold Ehlers (1930-1949). They celebrated 57 years of marriage when Walter passed away on March 15, 1980, in Eden, ID, at the age of 86. Olga followed him in death 10 years later on February 13, 1990, in Eden, at the age of 87. They were buried in Sunset Memorial Park Cemetery.

  3. Alvin Henry Meyerhoff (1903-1990)
    4. Alvin was the third child and second son of Clemens and Henrietta, born November 26, 1903, in Randolph County, IL. He married Hilda Anna Marie Werner (1903-1987) on November 5, 1929, in Clover, ID. Together they had four children: Patricia Ann Meyerhoff (1931-2007), Charlene Nadine (Meyerhoff) Lohnes (1933-2015), a living daughter in the late 1930s, and a living son in the early 1940s. They celebrated 57 years of marriage before Hilda passed away on February 15, 1987, in Kimberly, ID, at the age of 83. Alvin followed her in death three years later on May 31, 1990, in Eden, at the age of 86. They were buried in Sunset Memorial Park Cemetery.

  4. Raymond George Meyerhoff (1906-1991)
    5. Raymond was the fourth child and third son of Clemens and Henrietta, born August 26, 1906, in Wine Hill, IL. He married Emilena Elfriede Martens (1916-1998) on November 28, 1937, in Eden. They had four daughters and one son between 1938 and 1955, who are all living as of July 2024. Raymond worked as a farmer on the Meyerhoff farm in Eden his entire life. The couple celebrated 53 years of marriage when Raymond passed away on May 12, 1991, in Eden, at the age of 84. Emilena followed him in death seven years later on October 8, 1998, in Twin Falls, ID, at the age of 82. They were buried in Sunset Memorial Park Cemetery.

  5. Edna Caroline (Meyerhoff) Meyer (1911-1982)
    6. Edna was the fifth and last child and second daughter of Clemens and Henrietta, born August 9, 1911, in Chester, IL. She married German-born Gerhard William Meyer (1913-1973) on March 28, 1937, in Eden. Together they had three daughters and two sons, of whom all are still living except for John Henry Meyer (1948-2021). The couple celebrated 36 years of marriage when Gerhard passed away on April 22, 1973, in Eden, at the age of 60. Edna followed him in death 11 years later on April 11, 1982, in Eden, at the age of 70. They were buried in Sunset Memorial Park Cemetery.
