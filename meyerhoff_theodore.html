~ The Family of Theodore and Margaretha (Brandt) Meyerhoff ~

Theodore C. Meyerhoff was the third child of Carl and Emma (Walter) Meyerhoff, born Thursday, December 14, 1882, in Wine Hill, IL. He married Margaretha Agnes Brandt on April 21, 1912, in Campbell Hill, IL. Together they had seven children, listed below. After 36 years of marriage, Margaretha passed away on October 4, 1948, at the age of 55. Theodore died 13 years later on January 12, 1962, at the age of 79. Together they were buried in Saint Peter Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill.

Margaretha Agnes Brandt was born Monday, December 19, 1892, in Jackson County, IL. She was the daughter of Hinrich Brandt Sr. (1860-1945) and Mina (Golnick) Brandt (1867-1941). She was the couple's second child, and was one of nine children. She passed away on October 4, 1948, in Chester, IL.

Most of my Meyerhoff records come from U.S. census data. In 1900 and 1910, Theodore lived on the Meyerhoff farm in Wine Hill. He married Maggie in 1912 and they raised their family on a farm in the Southwestern Precinct of Perry County, IL. This is where the couple are found in the 1920, 1930, and 1940 censuses. In the 1950 census, Maggie had passed away, but Theodore (at 67 years old) still worked on the farm with his oldest son Edmund. Meanwhile, his other children on the farm had jobs at the Shoe Factory and brick plant.

  1. Lorene Emma Minna (Meyerhoff) Lohman (1913-1970)
    2. Lorene was the first child of Theodore and Margaretha, born February 6, 1913, in Willisville, IL. She married Louis Henry Lohman (1903-1990) on December 21, 1932, in Campbell Hill. Together they had 10 children: five daughters and five sons. Eight are still living at this time; the others are Delores A. (Lohman) Lohrding (1939-2012) and Ralph Frederick Lohman (1940-2017). The couple was married 37 years before Lorene passed away on January 26, 1970, at the age of 56. Louis followed her in death 20 years later on June 27, 1990, at the age of 86. Together they were buried in Saint John Evangelical Lutheran Cemetery in Chester, IL.

  2. Edmund Theodore Meyerhoff (1915-1992)
    3. Edmund was the second child and first son of Theodore and Margaretha, born February 12, 1915, in Willisville. I don't have any records of marriage or children; he passed away on January 2, 1992, at the age of 76. He was buried in Trinity Lutheran Church Cemetery in Red Bud, IL. My last record of Edmund is the 1950 census, where he lived with his father helping with the farm in Willisville.

  3. Henry Louis Meyerhoff (1917-1986)
    4. Henry was the third child and second son of Theodore and Margaretha, born March 10, 1917, in Willisville. He married Catherine Foster (1916-????) on February 9, 1950, in Osceola, AR. I don't have any records of children. Henry was a farmer. He passed away on January 31, 1986, in Belleville, IL, at the age of 68. He was buried in Trinity Lutheran Church Cemetery. I am not sure where Catherine is buried; it's possible they may have gotten divorced or she chose to be cremated and doesn't have any marker.

  4. Omer Henry Meyerhoff (1919-1985)
    5. Omer was the fourth child and third son of Theodore and Margaretha, born November 6, 1919, in Willisville. I don't have any records of marriage or children. In his lifetime he worked as a welder for A.O. Smith Co. He passed away on October 8, 1985, in Belleville, at the age of 65. He was buried in Trinity Lutheran Church Cemetery.

  5. Elmer Herman Meyerhoff (1922-1983)
    6. Elmer was the fifth child and fourth son of Theodore and Margaretha, born March 12, 1922, in Willisville. He served in the U.S. Army during World War II and married Dorothy Mae Stanka (nee Hornbostel) (1929-1999) on December 23, 1950. I have no records of any children. They celebrated 33 years of marriage when Elmer passed away on December 15, 1983, in Caseyville, IL, at the age of 61. Dorothy followed him in death on March 26, 1999, in Belleville, at the age of 69. They were buried in Valhalla Gardens of Memory and Mausoleum in Belleville.

  6. Gertrude Emilie (Meyerhoff) Kloepper (1924-2007)
    7. Gertrude was the sixth child and second daughter of Theodore and Margaretha, born September 4, 1924, in Percy, IL. She married Melvin August Kloepper (1924-1999) on June 4, 1955, in Campbell Hill, IL. They had three children together in the 1950s and 1960s, two sons and a daughter. They were married 44 years when Melvin passed away on June 14, 1999, in Belleville, at the age of 74. Gertrude followed him in death on March 20, 2007, in Chester, IL, at the age of 82. They were buried in Trinity Lutheran Church Cemetery.

  7. Evelyn Lucille (Meyerhoff) Kloepper (1928-1977)
    8. Evelyn was the seventh and last child and third daughter of Theodore and Margaretha, born July 3, 1928, in Willisville. She married Marvin Gottlieb Kloepper (1926-2004, cousin of Melvin Kloepper) on April 19, 1952, in Campbell Hill. Together they had two children in the mid-1950s. They celebrated 25 years of marriage before Evelyn passed away on September 26, 1977, in Red Bud, IL, at the age of 49. Marvin passed away 27 years later on April 8, 2004, in Red Bud, at the age of 77.
