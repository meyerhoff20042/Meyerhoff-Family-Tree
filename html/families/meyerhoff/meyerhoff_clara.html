~ The Family of August and Clara (Meyerhoff) Hoops ~

Clara Alvina Meyerhoff was the fourth child of Carl and Emma's union, and Carl's seventh child overall. She was born July 13, 1885, in Wine Hill, IL. She married August Herman Hoops (1884-1959) on April 27, 1911, in Wine Hill. Together they had eight children listed below. Clara passed away on November 2, 1970, in Chester, IL, at the age of 85, after a three-month illness.

August Herman Hoops was the son of Anna Hesterman (1854-1912) and Henry H. Hoops (1847-1923), born December 16, 1884, in Campbell Hill, IL. During his lifetime, he worked as a miner. He passed away on August 14, 1959, in Willisville, IL, at the age of 74, after a three-month illness.

The couple were buried in Saint Peter Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill, IL. August was buried on August 17, 1959, and Clara was buried on November 4, 1970.

  1. Raymond Hoops (Mar. 1912 - Jun. 1912)
    2. Raymond was the first child of Clara and August, born March 15, 1912. He unfortunately passed away after only three months on June 28, 1912. He was buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill.

  2. Anna Hoops (1913-1915)
    3. Anna was the second child and first daughter of Clara and August, born June 29, 1913. Like her brother, she passed away very young, on July 9, 1915, at the age of 2. She was buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery.

  3. Mary Esther Hoops (Jun. 1916 - Jul. 1916)
    4. Mary was the third child and second daughter of Clara and August, born June 22, 1916. She was the third child to die young, passing away on July 9, 1916, after only 17 days. She was buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery.

  4. Leona A. (Hoops) Rathert (1917-1973)
    5. Leona was the fourth child and third daughter of Clara and August, born June 21, 1917, in Willisville. She was the first of their children to survive childhood, and one of only three. She married Herbert Edward Rathert (1920-1991) on May 8, 1943, in Campbell Hill. Together they had at least two children, who are both living. They celebrated almost 30 years of marriage when Leona passed away on March 13, 1973, in Sparta, IL, at the age of 55, after a four-week illness. Herbert followed her in death 18 years later on September 8, 1991, in Chester, IL, at the age of 71. They were buried in Saint Peter Lutheran Cemetery.

  5. Irene Bertha (Hoops) Thies (1920-2006)
    6. Irene was the fifth child and fourth daughter of Clara and August, born June 2, 1920, in Willisville. She married Melvin Ernest "Barney" Thies, Sr. (1921-1962) on February 20, 1943, in Perryville, MO. Together they had 11 children, of whom seven are still living. The other four were Gerald Lee Thies (1943-2019), Kenneth Wayne "Cornbread" Thies (1946-2000), Lonnie Ray "Buck" Thies (1955-1998), and Randy Michael "Nip" Thies (1959-2018). The couple were married 19 years when Melvin died of a gunshot wound on September 26, 1962, at the young age of 40 (I'm not sure what caused that). Irene passed away 44 years later on October 25, 2006, in Campbell Hill, at the age of 86. They were buried in Saint Peter Lutheran Cemetery.

  6. Walter Hoops (1923-1928)
    7. Walter was the sixth child and second son of Clara and August, born March 2, 1923, in Willisville. He passed away on April 21, 1928, in Eden, ID, at only five years old. He was buried in Trinity Lutheran Church Cemetery in Eden, ID. As he was the only child to pass away during the family's brief stint in Idaho (with Clemens and Henry), he was the only one buried there.

  7. Norman Hoops (1924-1926)
    8. Norman was the seventh child and third son of Clara and August, born April 6, 1924, in Willisville. He passed away on May 4, 1926, in Willisville, at the young age of two. He was buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery.

  8. Ellanora (Hoops) Knop (1929-2016)
    9. Ellanora was the eighth and last child and fifth daughter of Clara and August, born December 22, 1929, in Eden, ID. She married Omer Knop (1926-2012) on November 7, 1948, in Campbell Hill. Together the couple had at least two children, who are both living. They celebrated 64 years of marriage when Omer passed away on October 8, 2012, in Red Bud, IL, at the age of 85. Ellanora followed him in death three years later on January 15, 2016, in Chester, at the age of 86. They were buried in Saint John Evangelical Lutheran Cemetery in Chester.
