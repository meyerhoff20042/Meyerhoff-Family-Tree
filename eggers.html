~ The Eggers Family ~

The Eggers family immigrated to the United States from Germany sometime between 1850 and 1880. Heinrich A. Eggers, the patriarch of this family, is the oldest confirmed relative I have on this side of the tree. Born December 24, 1849, in Sultan, Prussia, he is believed to have been the son of Marie Steinke and Hans Heinrich Eggers. Heinrich married Sophie Margaretha Lehnhoff (1859-1927) on November 8, 1878, in Campbell Hill, IL, and together they had seven children listed below. Heinrich passed away on January 26, 1922, in Campbell Hill, at the age of 72. Sophie followed him in death five years later on February 22, 1927, in Randolph County, at the age of 67. They were buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill.

  1. Sophia (Eggers) Schuette (1879-1962)
    2. Sophia was the first child of Heinrich and Sophie, born August 20, 1879, in Campbell Hill. She married Henry Schuette (1866-1948) on September 2, 1897, in Campbell Hill, and together they had six children: Alma (Schuette) Alms (1898-1967), Clara (Schuette) Heinks (1900-1927), Herman Schuette (1903-1965), Albert Schuette (1908-1925), Frieda (Schuette) Krantz (1911-2003), and Erna (Schuette) Biermann (1918-2008). Henry passed away on June 23, 1948, in Randolph County, at the age of 81. Sophia followed him in death exactly 14 years later on June 23, 1962, in Randolph County, at the age of 82. They were buried in Saint Peter's Lutheran Cemetery in Wine Hill.

  2. Bertha A. (Eggers) Ohlau (1884-1967)
    3. Bertha was the second child of Heinrich and Sophie, born August 30, 1884, in Jackson County, IL. She married Hermann Friedrich Ohlau (1882-1944) on November 5, 1905, in Campbell Hill. Together they had four children: Anna (Ohlau) Clasen (1906-1983), Arthur Ohlau (1909-2007), Edgar Ohlau (1917-1986), and Melvin Ohlau (1926-2000). Hermann passed away November 2, 1944, in Murphysboro, IL, at the age of 62. Bertha followed him in death 23 years later on December 5, 1967, in Percy, IL, at the age of 83. They were buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill.

  3. Frieda (Eggers) Wachsnicht (1888-1966)
    4. Frieda was the third child of Heinrich and Sophie, born May 20, 1888, in Campbell Hill. She married Fred "Fritz" Wachsnicht (1891-1949) on October 16, 1913, in Jackson County. Together they had five children: Anna (Wachsnicht) Hornbostel (1914-1991), Fred Wachsnicht (1917-1977), Esther Wachsnicht (May 1921 - Jun. 1921), Homer Wachsnicht (1923-1934), and Leroy Wachsnicht (November 3-27, 1928). Fritz passed away on June 27, 1949, in Degognia, IL, at the age of 57. Frieda followed him in death 17 years later on January 21, 1966, in Chester, IL, at the age of 77. They were buried in Saint Luke's Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill.

  4. Emma Louise Wilhelmine (Eggers) Meyerhoff (1890-1970)
    5. Emma was the fourth and middle child of Heinrich and Sophie, born May 22, 1890, in Randolph County. She married Adolph Heinrich Wilhelm August Meyerhoff (1880-1967) on May 25, 1911, in Jackson County. Together they had nine children: Raymond Meyerhoff (1912-2004), Edna (Meyerhoff) Wettig (1914-2002), Alfred Meyerhoff (1916-2001), Albert Meyerhoff (1917-2004), Irene (Meyerhoff) Steffens (1919-2010), Infant Son Meyerhoff (born and died December 25, 1922), Esther (Meyerhoff) Beckman (1924-2017), Ruby Meyerhoff (1927-1931), and Verna (Meyerhoff) Golding (1931-1992). Adolph passed away July 11, 1967, in Anna, IL, at the age of 86. Emma followed him in death three years later on January 30, 1970, in Wine Hill, at the age of 79. They were buried in Saint Paul Lutheran Cemetery in Wine Hill.

  5. Georg Heinrich Hermann Eggers (1894-1900)
    6. Georg was the fifth child and first son of Heinrich and Sophie, born January 1, 1894, in Randolph County. He died November 4, 1900, at the young age of 6. He was buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery in Campbell Hill.

  6. Anna F. (Eggers) Stromeyer (1899-1987)
    7. Anna was the sixth child of Heinrich and Sophie, born June 3, 1899, in Percy, IL. She married Herbert D. Stromeyer (1893-1986) on April 29, 1923, in Campbell Hill. Together they had one son, Norman Stromeyer (1923-2001). Herbert passed away on December 9, 1986, in Percy, at the age of 93. Anna followed him in death one year later on September 5, 1987, in Carbondale, IL, at the age of 88. They were buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery.

  7. Ferd L. W. Eggers (1902-1984)
    8. Ferd was the seventh and final child of Heinrich and Sophie, born February 12, 1902, in Percy. He married Elda Gerberding (1910-1988) on May 3, 1928, in Campbell Hill. I do not have records of any children. Ferd passed away on February 15, 1984, in Percy, at the age of 82. Elda followed him in death four years later on January 12, 1988, in Percy, at the age of 77. They were buried in Saint John Lutheran Cemetery.
