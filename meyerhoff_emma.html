~ The Family of Herman and Emma (Meyerhoff) Joost ~

Emma Sophia Meyerhoff was the sixth and last child of Carl and Emma's union, and Carl's ninth and last child overall. She was born July 25, 1890, in Wine Hill, IL. She married Herman Henry Joost (1887-1961) on September 14, 1913, in Wine Hill. Together they had two children listed below. She passed away on December 15, 1982, in Percy, IL, at the age of 92.

Herman Henry Joost was the child of Wilhelmine "Minnie" Nettenrich (1858-1908) and August Joost (1857-1941). He was born November 8, 1887, in Shiloh Hill, IL. He and Emma were married 47 years until he passed away on January 2, 1961, in Steeleville, at the age of 73. He was working on a well and pulled out a faulty pump, and drowned in the well.

The couple were buried in Saint Peter's Lutheran Cemetery in Wine Hill. Herman was buried on January 5, 1961, and Emma was buried on December 18, 1982.

  1. Erna A. Joost (1919-2011)
    2. Erna was the first child and only daughter of Emma and Herman, born August 26, 1919, in Wine Hill. She never married. She worked as a laborer in the shoe factory (presumably International Shoe Factory). She passed away on May 24, 2011, in Sparta, IL, at the age of 91. She was buried in Saint Peter's Lutheran Cemetery.

  2. Elmer August Joost (1926-2004)
    3. Elmer was the second and last child and only son of Emma and Herman, born April 3, 1926, in Percy, IL. He served in the U.S. Army during World War II. He married Ida Marie Beyer (1923-2000) on September 21, 1957, in Wine Hill. Together they had at least two children, who are still living. They celebrated 42 years of marriage when Ida passed away on January 28, 2000, in Percy, at the age of 76. Elmer followed her in death four years later on July 13, 2004, in Percy, at the age of 78. They were buried in Saint Peter's Lutheran Cemetery.
