~ History of the British Royal Family ~

List of British Rulers

The table below is a list of all monarchs of Wessex, England, Great Britain, and the United Kingdom. It will show their name, house/dynasty, reign, and their relation to the monarch before them (if applicable). This list will skip over the English Commonwealth in the 17th century as the Cromwells ruled as "Lord Protectors" instead of kings, and had no connections to the royal family.
